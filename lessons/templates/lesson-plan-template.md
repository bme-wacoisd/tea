## Lesson Sequence

### Opening (5 minutes)
- Welcome and lesson overview
- Connect to prior knowledge or real-world relevance
- Present learning objectives

### Direct Instruction (8-10 minutes)
- Present slides covering key concepts
- Read aloud passage while students follow along
- Check for understanding with brief questions

### Guided Practice - Group Discussion (12-15 minutes)
- Organize students into small groups (3-4 students)
- Students access discussion prompts on their devices
- Students discuss prompts verbally, summarize on paper
- Instructor circulates to:
  - Listen to conversations
  - Ask probing questions
  - Provide scaffolding as needed
  - Redirect off-topic discussions

### Closure (3-5 minutes)
- Invite groups to share key insights
- Summarize main takeaways
- Transition to assessment

### Assessment (5-8 minutes)
- Students complete Google Classroom quiz individually
- Quiz settings: randomized questions, no retakes, hidden answers
