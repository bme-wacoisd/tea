# Group Discussion: Grouping Students for Learning

## Prompt 1: One Person Does All the Work
Think of a time you were in a group where one person did most of the work while others coasted. What went wrong? What structures or teacher moves could have prevented this?

## Prompt 2: The Ability Grouping Debate
What are the arguments FOR ability grouping (students of similar level together)? What are the arguments AGAINST it? Where do you personally stand, and why?

## Prompt 3: Different Tasks, Different Groups
How might you group students differently for a class discussion versus a hands-on science project versus independent reading? Why would the grouping change?

## Prompt 4: The Quiet Student
A student never speaks during whole-class discussions but comes alive in small groups. What does this tell you about the relationship between grouping and participation? How might you use this information?
