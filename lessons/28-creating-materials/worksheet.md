# Group Discussion: Creating Materials That Work

## Prompt 1: Confusing Materials
Think of a worksheet or handout from your own experience that was confusing or frustrating. What made it hard to use? What would have made it better?

## Prompt 2: Adapt or Replace?
You find a worksheet online that's almost what you need, but not quite. How do you decide whether it's worth adapting versus finding or creating something different? What factors influence this decision?

## Prompt 3: Teach or Decorate?
What's the difference between a visual that teaches and a visual that just decorates? Think of an example of each. How can you tell whether an image or graphic is adding to learning or distracting from it?

## Prompt 4: Same Objective, Different Levels
You're teaching a lesson on comparing two characters from a novel. Some students read at grade level; some are below; some are above. How might you differentiate the worksheet or graphic organizer for students at different reading levels while keeping the same learning objective?
