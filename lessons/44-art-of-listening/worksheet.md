# Group Discussion: The Art of Listening

## Prompt 1: Feeling Heard
Think of a time someone really listened to you. What did they do that made you feel heard? What was different about that conversation?

## Prompt 2: Listening to Respond vs. Listening to Understand
What's the difference between listening to respond and listening to understand? How can you tell which one you're doing?

## Prompt 3: The Angry Parent
A parent is angry about their child's grade. Why might you listen fully before explaining your reasoning? What happens if you start defending immediately?

## Prompt 4: Beyond Nodding
What makes paraphrasing and reflecting feelings more useful than just nodding and saying "uh-huh"?

## Prompt 5: Your Listening Barriers
What listening barriers are hardest for you personally to overcome? Multitasking? Preparing your response? Judging? Problem-solving too soon?

## Prompt 6: Listening to Students vs. Adults
How does active listening with students differ from active listening with adults? What adjustments might you make?
