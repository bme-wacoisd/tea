**Answer Key Summary** (for instructor reference only):
1. B - They felt confident but performed worse on tests
2. B - It forces students to notice the collision between their beliefs and reality

4. B - False
5. B - When what students believed meets what is actually true
6. B - Ask a question or reveal a common misconception
7. B - A way of noticing when your intuitions are wrong and updating them
8. B - Ask students what they already think is true before explaining
