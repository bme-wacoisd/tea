# Group Discussion: Digital Citizenship

## Prompt 1: Native vs. Citizen
What's the difference between being a "digital native" (someone who grew up with technology) and being a "digital citizen" (someone who uses technology responsibly)? Can you be one without the other?

## Prompt 2: Modeling Matters
Why is it important for teachers to MODEL digital citizenship, not just teach about it? What happens when teachers violate the rules they teach?

## Prompt 3: Age-Appropriate Teaching
Choose one element of digital citizenship (etiquette, security, literacy, etc.). How would you teach it to elementary students versus high school students? What would change?

## Prompt 4: Teacher's Digital Life
How might a teacher's personal social media use affect their professional life? What should teachers consider about their own digital footprint?
