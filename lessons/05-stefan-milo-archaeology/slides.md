# Slides: Stefan Milo — Teaching Archaeology and Anthropology

## Planning Notes

This lesson teaches future educators how to teach about human origins and prehistory using Milo's approach:
- Define terms precisely from the start (archaeology, anthropology, prehistory, hominin)
- Academic rigor: "No Atlantis, No Aliens, No Nonsense"
- Make ancient people REAL—not just facts about them
- Use evidence to tell human stories, not just data

## Slide Flow

1. **Title**: Stefan Milo - Archaeology and Anthropology
