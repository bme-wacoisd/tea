**Answer Key Summary** (for instructor reference only):
1. B - With a concrete example or visualization
2. C - Art, fiction, and imagination
3. B - Watching builds intuition but doesn't replace doing the work
4. B - False
5. B - Intuition—a mental picture of what's really happening
6. B - Definition → Theorem → Proof → Examples
7. B - The formula describes what you already understand after seeing it
8. B - Physical objects like blocks and shapes
