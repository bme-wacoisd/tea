# Group Discussion: Student Engagement

## Prompt 1: Compliance vs. Engagement
What's the difference between a compliant student and an engaged student? How can you tell them apart?

## Prompt 2: Your Own Engagement
Think of a time you were deeply engaged in learning something. What made it engaging? Can you identify which factors were present?

## Prompt 3: "This Is Boring"
A student says "This is boring." What might actually be happening? What could you try to increase engagement?

## Prompt 4: The Challenge Zone
How do you create appropriate challenge when students are at different levels? What happens when work is too easy? Too hard?

## Prompt 5: Measuring Engagement
Can engagement be measured? What would you look for? How would you know if your lesson was engaging?

## Prompt 6: Technology and Engagement
How might technology designed to make learning "easier" actually reduce engagement? When does technology increase engagement, and when does it decrease it?
