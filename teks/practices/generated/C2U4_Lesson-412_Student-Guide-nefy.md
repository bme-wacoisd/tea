#### Practice #1

Examine the following sample student-teacher interactions:
Interaction #1:
Teacher: Use the word "reassured" in a sentence.
Student: My mother reassured me that we were safe inside the house during the storm.
Interaction #2:
Teacher: What are the three branches of the federal government?
Student: The three branches of the federal government are executive, judicial, and legislative.


Script two Non-Directive prompts you might give for each interaction.
