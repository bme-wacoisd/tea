## Exit Ticket - Answer Key

### Lesson 10.10: Professionalism, Presentation, and Conduct

Name:
Date:
Mrs. R. is working in her classroom after-school when one of her students stops by. The student explains that he “can’t understand” the way that his math teacher explains the content, but that he can “always understand” how Mrs. R. teaches. He asks Mrs. R. to help him with his math homework. Mrs. R. starts to assist him and explains that his math teacher is new and probably still struggling with how to teach well.
How did Mrs. R. act professionally?
It is professional for her to do her best to help students, even with work outside of her class assignments.
How did Mrs. R. act unprofessionally?
Mrs. R. should not have said that the student’s math teacher is new and still struggling to learn how to teach.
How could Mrs. R. have acted more professionally?
They could have encouraged the student to seek help from their math teacher and explain that they are having trouble understanding the content.
