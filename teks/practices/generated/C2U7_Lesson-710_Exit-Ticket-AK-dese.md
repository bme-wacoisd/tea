How did the QAR strategy help you to develop deeper questions?
The QAR strategy helped me to understand the relationship between a question and its answer.  The strategy helped me see that in order to generate quality questions, a student must make connections between the question, the text, and their own background knowledge.

| Question | Question Type |
| --- | --- |
| Where does the story take place? | Author and Me |
| Whose idea was it to play the rhyme game? | Right There |
| What words did Bella have to create rhymes with? | Think and Search |
| Why did Bella keep laughing at James’ rhymes? | Author and Me |
