## Slide 3

### Bellringer

- Consider the following scenarios in a 5th grade classroom. Ms. K stands upright at the front of the classroom. Speaking in a calm, low voice, she says, “Pass in your papers.”
- Ms. J sits at her desk in the corner of the room. Uses a high-pitched fast voice, she yells, “Pass in your papers!”
- How would you expect the students to react to each teacher? Based only on this scenario, what conclusions do you make about the culture in each classroom?

## Slide 4

### Vocabulary


| Strong Voice | a technique for adjusting your speech and body language to ensure students listen carefully to what you say |
| --- | --- |
| Speech & Body Language | communication using spoken words, facial expressions, posture, eye contact, and gestures |
| Formal Register | tenor of communication with controlled body language and a warm but serious tone |
| Modulate | vary the strength, tone, or pitch of one's voice |
