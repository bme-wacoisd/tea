Presentation Checklist

Final Review and Practice of Portfolio Presentation
Directions:  Use the next few minutes to silently review and practice your Portfolio Presentation. Refer to the “Components of an Effective Presentation” and/or your Checklist above to make any small changes or revisions.
Components of an Effective Presentation:
- Organization and Structure: The presentation is developed in a focused, creative, and coherent way.
- Content: The presentation addresses all required components and provides appropriate elaboration around each element.
- Design: The presentation is visually appealing, using colors, images, and graphics to strengthen the quality of the final product.
- Language and Delivery: The presenter speaks clearly, effectively, and confidently (e.g., volume, pace, articulation) in a presentation that does not exceed 10 minutes.
Portfolio Presentation Norms
As you are an audience member of Portfolio Presentations, please use the following Norms:
- Listen actively
- Remain engaged and focused on the presenter
- Respect the presenter
- Raise your hand to ask relevant questions
