### Vocabulary

| --- | --- |
| Demeanor | Behavior toward others; outward manner |
| Consistency | When a teacher always follows through on classroom expectations or procedures. |
| High Expectations | Actions based on the belief that all students can achieve at high levels for academics and behavior |
