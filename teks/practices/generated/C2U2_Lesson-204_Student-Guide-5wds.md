| Register | Speech | Body Language & Facial Expression | Appropriate as a Classroom Teacher for |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Casual | Volume varies; cadence varies; pitch varies; pace may be faster | Asymmetrical or relaxed body posture; inconsistent eye contact; sweeping hand gestures |  |
| Formal | Quieter volume; consistent cadence with less variation in tone; clear articulation of syllables, and distinction between words; lower pitch; slower pace | Symmetrical posture; straight back; steady eye contact; chin up; hands mostly still (e.g., clasped) |  |
| Urgent | Higher volume; consistent cadence with words likely running together; higher pitch; fast pace | Wide eyes; leaning in; sharp gestures |  |
