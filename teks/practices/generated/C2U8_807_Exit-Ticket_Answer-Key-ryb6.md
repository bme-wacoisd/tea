A PE teacher demonstrates how to effectively complete a free throw in basketball while explaining his process out loud.

Audio and visual


How might the teacher add two additional modalities to the lesson?

The teacher could pass out a written summary of the steps to complete the free throw. The teacher could have students physically practice the elements of a free throw.
