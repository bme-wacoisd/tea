## Slide 4

### Vocabulary

- Stretch It: Respond to students’ correct answers with more challenging questions
- Directive Prompts: Question prompts to guide students to think further in a specific way
- Non-Directive Prompts: Phrases to push students to further develop an idea without suggesting what to add
- 4
