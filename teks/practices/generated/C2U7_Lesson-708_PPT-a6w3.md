## Slide 4

### Vocabulary

- Protocol: A well-defined set of actions used in a classroom for a specific academic purpose.
- 4

---

## Slide 5

### Roles for Protocols

- Protocols enhance the structure or clarity of an open-ended task such as brainstorming or generating ideas.
- Protocols promote equal participation by encouraging students to take turns between listening and speaking.
- Protocols assign clear roles and tasks so opportunities for collaborative work are maximized.
- 5

---

## Slide 6

### Protocol Similarities

- Protocols are organized and have identified what participants will do.
- Protocols have timeframes for each step and specify how long participants have to complete each step.
- Protocols include a description of what the protocol will do to structure learning.
