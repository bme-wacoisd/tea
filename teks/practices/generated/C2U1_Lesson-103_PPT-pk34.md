## Slide 4

### Vocabulary

- Procedure: The step-by-step process for completing a recurring action in the classroom
- Routine: Procedures and systems that are automatic and habitual
- System: A set of related procedures that helps a classroom run smoothly and efficiently
- 4

## Slide 5

### Types of Routines

- Academic Routines: Routines that “enable students to complete tasks that directly support learning, like how to annotate a text or make eye contact during a classroom discussion” (Texas TLAC Online)
- Behavioral Routines: Routines that “help students manage themselves, their space, and their materials while maximizing efficiency” (Texas TLAC Online)
- Cultural Routines: Routines that “give students clear actions to demonstrate their shared classroom values, norms, and aspirations” (Texas TLAC Online)
