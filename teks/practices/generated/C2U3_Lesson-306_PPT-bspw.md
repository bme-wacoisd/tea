## Slide 4

### Vocabulary

- Asset-Based Teaching: Approach to teaching that focuses on what students can do rather than what they cannot do.
- Deficit-Based Teaching: Approach to teaching that focuses on what students cannot do rather than what they can do.
- Holding High Expectations: Believing that all students can learn at high-levels and teaching in a way that reflects that belief.
