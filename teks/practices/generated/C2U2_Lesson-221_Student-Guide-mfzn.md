Practice 1: Nonverbal Interventions Scenarios

In groups, engage with the following TLAC scenarios to practice common nonverbal interventions.  Focus on delivering a nonverbal intervention that communicates the solution while using emotional constancy.  After each student has the opportunity to practice during each scenario, group members will provide warm and cool feedback.
