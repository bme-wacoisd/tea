### Lesson 2.27: Classroom Management Plans

Name:
Date:
- What should be included in an effective classroom management plan?  Why?

- All aspects of classroom management, including strategies for the development of positive relationships, proactive and responsive techniques for behavior management and intervention, physical classroom design, and classroom rules, systems, and procedures, should be included in a classroom management plan.



- Why should teachers use a classroom management plan?

- A successful classroom management plan permits the cultivation and sustention of an effective learning environment.
