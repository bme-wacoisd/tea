| Paragraph Structure | The paragraph should have at least 5 complete sentences. The paragraph should include an introduction sentence and a closing sentence. |
| --- | --- |
| Punctuation & Capitalization | Sentences should end with the appropriate punctuation. Proper nouns and the beginning words of sentences should be consistently capitalized. |
| Supporting Details | At least three supporting details are included in the paragraph. |
| Drawing | The drawing appropriately represents some of the details in the paragraph, adding details and interest. |
| Descriptive Title | The paragraph should include a descriptive title. |


|  | Examples of Effective Feedback Statements | Non-Examples |
