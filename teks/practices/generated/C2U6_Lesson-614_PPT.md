## Slide 4

### Vocabulary


| Curriculum | A curriculum is a year-long, standards-based guide of instructional practices, learning experiences, and assessments used to facilitate student learning of grade-level concepts. |
| --- | --- |
| Objectives | Brief statements that describe what students will be expected to learn by the end of the unit or lesson |
| Assessment | Any tool used to collect, analyze, and respond to information about student understanding of learning targets |
| Lesson Plan | A teacher’s detailed plan for teaching an individual lesson |
| Norms | Agreements adopted by a group for how it will do something together and/or behave together. |
