## Slide 4

### Benefits of 1:1 Conferences

- 4
- They allow teachers to build on the relationships that they have with students.
- They provide an opportunity for teachers to address individual misunderstandings,
- They boost student confidence and reduce anxieties.
- They provide a regular opportunity for students to share their concerns

---

## Slide 5

### Practices for Setting Up 1:1 Conferences

- 5
- Schedule conferences regularly and keep them short (5 minutes or less)
- Before the conference, plan out what you will share as strengths/areas of improvement. Also have the students reflect on their strengths/struggles before the conference.
- Share your main thoughts on their progress/work.  Keep your thoughts to three points or less, starting with the positive.
- End with a goal that can be focused on for the next conference.
