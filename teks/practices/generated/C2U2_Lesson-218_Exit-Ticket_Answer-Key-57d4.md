Analyze the following scene from a classroom: “Radar/Be Seen Looking.” Respond to the following questions and provide specific evidence from the video to support your answers.

- How did the teacher effectively use the Radar technique?

- He provided specific and concrete instructions for the students; he scanned the classroom after each direction; he scanned from the corner – Pastore’s Perch.




- How did the teacher use Radar to communicate to students that he was carefully observing their actions?

- He positioned himself in an area of the room where all students could see him, he scanned the room and then acknowledged specific students for following the directions.




- How did the teacher’s actions promote a positive and effective classroom environment?

The teacher’s actions communicated high expectations for his students; he promoted a safe and orderly environment that supports learning, he promoted a space where students knew could visibly see he was watching them and care about their success; he communicated accountability.
