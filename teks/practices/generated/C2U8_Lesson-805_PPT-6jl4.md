## Slide 9

### One on One Conferences

- A conversation between a teacher and an individual student focused on the student’s learning progress.
- Criteria for one-on-one student conferences:
- Are brief
- Include attention to the students’ emotions about learning
- Provide scaffolded opportunity for student reflection on their own learning progress
- Focus on the student’s strengths and opportunities for growth
- Include goal-setting
- End with next steps
