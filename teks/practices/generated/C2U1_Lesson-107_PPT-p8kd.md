## Slide 4

### Vocabulary

- Engineer Efficiency: Create a process that decreases the amount of effort needed to complete a task.
- Double Planned: A plan that includes all details about what a teacher and students will do.
- Minimally Narrated: Using the fewest number of words possible while maintaining clarity.
- 4

## Slide 5

### Three Criteria for Effective Routines

- Effective routines must be:
- Simple
- Well planned (double planned)
- Minimally narrated
