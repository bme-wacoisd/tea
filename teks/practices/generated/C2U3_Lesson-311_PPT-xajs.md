### Bellringer

- Read the scenario below and respond to the question.
- A new student (Tom) has enrolled in Ms. G’s seventh grade class. He used to attend a different school in the same school district. He has been quiet since he joined the class.
- What are some strategies that Ms. G can use to find out more about Tom? Think back to our lesson on building relationships with students to develop your list.

- Circle true or false for each of the statements in your Student Guide. If you circle false, explain your reasoning underneath the statement.

- List a question answered by quantitative and qualitative data. For each type, give an example of what a teacher might gather.
