### Lesson 10.03: The Qualities of Effective Schools | What Makes and Effective School?

Name:
Date:
Bellringer:
Schools provide the environment in which teachers and students operate, and each school has certain characteristics that can help us determine if they are effective in helping students and teachers find success. For example, effective schools provide teachers and students with high-quality instructional materials.
Use the chart below to brainstorm qualities of effective schools based on the categories provided.


Instruction: The Texas Effective Schools Framework Notes

Practice: ESF Essential Actions Stations
