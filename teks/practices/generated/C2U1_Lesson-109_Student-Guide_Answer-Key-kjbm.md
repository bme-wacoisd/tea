Describe a time when you were told by a parent or a teacher to do something, but no one told you why you needed to do it. What was your response? How did the situation feel?
Responses may vary but may include:

Discussion and Notes: Student Ownership in Systems and Routines
Student ownership: students support and want an idea or process in the classroom to be successful
Buy-in: acceptance and willingness to put an idea or process into practice
Roll-out speech: public explanation of an element of a classroom and its importance to the classroom culture
