Scenario:
An English teacher asks her class to write a short essay that recounted the three most important events that happened during the weekend.  She modeled the process with a worked example of the steps shown below sharing how each step contributes to the process:
- Write a list of all the activities you did during the weekend.
- Rank the activities from most important to least important.
- Identify two fun facts about each of the top three activities.
- Use these fun facts to craft short sentences about the activities that can be combined to create the essay.
The teacher began by modeling the process by sharing her planning notes and sample essay, which the class reviewed together.  She annotated the steps on her notes and essay as she reviewed it with the class.  The class was then asked to follow the steps to craft their sentences for their essay.  Prior to writing the full essay, the students shared their work with a partner to get feedback.  They then wrote the brief essay.
