## Slide 4

### Vocabulary

- Task: Things that a student must do in a class to learn
- Rigorous: Tasks that require students to engage in effortful thinking to deepen their understanding of the content
- 4

---

## Slide 5

### Guidelines for Rigorous Tasks

- Build on and extend prior knowledge
- Involve effortful thought
- Deepen conceptual understanding of material
- Require learners to communicate their understanding to others
- Involve multiple interpretations or paths to a solution
- Content of Thinking: Rigorous Tasks. Deans for Impact. Retrieved from https://deansforimpact.org/content-of-thinking/rigorous-tasks/
