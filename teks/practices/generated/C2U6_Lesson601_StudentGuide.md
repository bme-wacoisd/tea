High-Quality Instructional Materials
In Texas, high-quality instructional materials (HQIM) are research-based, aligned to the TEKS, differentiated, and reviewed by a team of experts.

A high-quality curriculum is a tool that, when used properly by a well-prepared teacher, can have a significant positive impact on student learning.

The Components of a Curriculum
A curriculum is a sequenced collection of instructional materials such as lesson plans, learning tasks, and assessments. Teachers use it to guide students toward proficiency in content knowledge and skills.
