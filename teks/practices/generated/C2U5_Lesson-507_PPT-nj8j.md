## Slide 5

### Wait Time

- Wait time is a period of time between when a teacher poses a question and when students are given the opportunity to answer.
- Wait time provides an opportunity for all students to consider the answer (not only the fastest thinkers) and can improve the quality of responses.
- Teachers should wait at least five seconds between the time they ask a question and the time they call on or allow a student to answer.
