## Slide 4

### Vocabulary


| Be Seen Looking | A strategy of non-verbal communication in which a teacher uses gestures or moves (tilt of the head, a nod, etc.) to cause students to notice that the teacher is a good and careful observer |
| --- | --- |
| Radar | A classroom management strategy in which the teacher gives clear, observable directions, visually scans the room, and positions themself to be easily seen by students |
| Scanning | Looking consistently and intentionally across the entire classroom |
| Pastore’s Perch | Observing from the corner of the room where you can see most effectively and proficiently. |
