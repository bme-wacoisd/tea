### Vocabulary

- Pace: Speed at which someone speaks
- Cadence: Rhythm of someone’s speech
- Pitch: Highness or lowness of someone’s voice
- Volume : Loudness or quietness of someone’s voice
- Tone: The accent or inflection of speech that indicates mood or emotion
