## Slide 5

### Scenario Practice: Example 1

- Middle school teachers have 10 minutes with their students in between lunch and the next class. Mrs. Sherron uses the time to read a book aloud to students. She insists that students do not talk during this time but allows them to draw or put their heads down to rest.
- What is Mrs. Sherron doing well?
- What can Mrs. Sherron do better?
