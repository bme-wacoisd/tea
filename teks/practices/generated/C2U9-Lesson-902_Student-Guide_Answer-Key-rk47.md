What is informal feedback?  What is the instructional purpose for a teacher to use informal feedback in the classroom?
Informal feedback is provided instantaneously in the moment to address specific needs.  It is not provided consistently to all students.  Informal feedback is important because it helps to correct mistakes quickly and to get the learning back on track.

The following prompts could be helpful as you are providing feedback:
