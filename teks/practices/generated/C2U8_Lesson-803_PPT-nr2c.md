### Bellringer

- Complete the following true/false statements and be ready to explain the reasoning for your choice.
- Every student should complete the same work.
- There is one form of engagement that will work for every student.
- Culture, personal relevance, and background knowledge influences a student’s engagement.
- All students should have the same resources and tools to complete a task.
- Students need a safe and supportive environment where they feel safe to learn and take risks.
- All students like working in groups.
