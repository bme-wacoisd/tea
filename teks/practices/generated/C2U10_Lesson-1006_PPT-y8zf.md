### Vocabulary

- 4

| Term | Definition |
| --- | --- |
| Scholarship | Money that does not need to be repaid based on academic merit, talent, or particular area of study |
| Student Loan | Money borrowed from a bank to attend a college or career school that must be repaid, usually with interest |
| Student Loan Forgiveness | A program where the government may forgive part or all of your federal student loans |
| Grant | A form of financial aid that doesn’t have to be repaid unless you do not fulfill the necessary requirements |
