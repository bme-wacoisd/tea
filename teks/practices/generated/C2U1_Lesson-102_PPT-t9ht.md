- What is most important for others to know about you?

- Who is in your family?
- What are your favorite hobbies and pastimes outside of school?

## Slide 4

### Ice Breakers

- Icebreaker: An activity that helps “warm up” conversation among a group of people.
- Ice Breaker Examples:
- Asking students and the teacher to answer questions about themselves and share their answers
- Asking students and the teacher to share about their names
- Asking students and the teacher to share eight nouns that describe them
- Asking students and the teacher to share five pictures that show what is important to them

## Slide 5

### Classroom Culture

- Systems and Routines:
- clear and consistent structures in a classroom that help students execute recurring activities correctly and efficiently
- Rapport:
- a sense of understanding between people
- Respect:
- regard for the feelings, wishes, rights, or traditions of others
- Belonging:
- a sense of being seen and accepted by a group
- Classroom Culture:
- the environment and tone of a classroom that impacts how students feel during instruction
