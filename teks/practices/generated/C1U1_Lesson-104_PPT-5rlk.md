## Slide 4

### Vocabulary

- Transition: The space and time between instructional activities during which teachers and students make a shift from one activity to another.
- Sequential: Occurring in a specific order
- Observable: Able to be seen
- 4

---

## Slide 5

### Characteristics of Effective Directions

- 5
- Specific
- Effective directions describe concrete actions that students should take
- Sequential
- Set out the order in which actions should be taken
- Observable
- The teacher needs to be able to see the students following the directions
