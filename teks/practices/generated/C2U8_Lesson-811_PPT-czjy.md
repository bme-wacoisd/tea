## Slide 10

### Practice Scenario: Mrs. D

- Mrs. D.’s students are learning about frogs and the life cycle of a frog. Students are working in groups to research and describe each stage of the frog’s life cycle. Each group has access to eBooks with the text-to-speech function, a touchscreen tablet, a laptop, and access to digital icons on their learning management system that link to specific kid-friendly websites and videos.
- Mrs. D. has asked students to record their learning in a graphic organizer using Google Docs. Before placing students in groups, Mrs. D. modeled how to use the voice typing function to help those who have difficulty typing and/or spelling.
