## Slide 5

### Characteristics of Effective Assessments

- Assessment is aligned with learning target(s) and measures appropriate levels of rigor based on learning target (aligned to and requires attention to the most important content)
- Assessment is fair and transparent (matches expectations of learning focus, not a “gotcha”)
- Assessment is practical (time, resources, student numbers)
- Assessment is cognitively engaging (why and how)
