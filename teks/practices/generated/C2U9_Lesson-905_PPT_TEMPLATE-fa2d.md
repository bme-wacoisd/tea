## Slide 4

### Key Practices for Planning a 1:1 Conference

- 4
- Keep the conferences short (5 minutes or less)
- Have students reflect on their own ahead of the conference
- Share both areas of strength and areas for improvement during the conference

---

## Slide 5

### Steps for 1:1 Conferences

- 5
- Start by greeting the student and briefly explain how the conference will go.
- Invite students to share their thoughts or how they feel about the task/assignment first.
- Share your main thoughts on their progress/work.  Keep your thoughts to 3 points or less, sharing strengths first.
- Ask the student if they have questions or need clarifications.
- Provide the student with a goal to work on after the conference and thank them for their time.
- Have students reflect individually on the process of the conference and what they learned/heard during the conference.
