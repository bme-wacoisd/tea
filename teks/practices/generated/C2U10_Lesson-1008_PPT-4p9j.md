## Slide 4

### Vocabulary

- Continuous Improvement Cycle: An intentional approach to improving one’s practice through planning, action, and reflection.
- Texas Lesson Study: A continuous improvement cycle in which Texas teachers work collaboratively to plan, practice, and reflect on instruction around a particular problem of practice within their content and grade level.

## Slide 5

### Plan-Do-Study-Act

- An example of a continuous improvement cycle where, when used in education, teachers plan for, implement, and observe the process of a desired change.
- 5

---

## Slide 6

### Plan

- Identify the problem
- List possible steps to approach the problem
- List desired outcomes
- 6

---

## Slide 7

### Do

- Apply the steps listed in the plan
- Take notes/make observations during this time
- 7

---

## Slide 8

### Study

- Study the results after applying the plan.
- Did you see the outcome you expected?
- What were the actual results when compared to the desired ones?
- 8

---

## Slide 9

### Act

- Consider your next steps.
- Has the solution addressed the problem?
- Do you need to return to any of the previous step?
