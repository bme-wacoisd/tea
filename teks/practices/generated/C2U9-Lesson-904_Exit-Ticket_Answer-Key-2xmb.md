What are the benefits of using 1:1 conferences to provide formal feedback?
1:1 conferences all the feedback to be two way, which can reduce misunderstandings about the content or feedback.  Additionally, 1:1 conferences can build student confidence and reduce anxiety.

What are some practices that teachers should consider when planning 1:1 conferences with students?
The conferences should be short (5 min or less), students should reflect on the task ahead of the conference, should include strengths and areas for improvement as well as identify goals for the next conference.
