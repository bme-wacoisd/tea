| Assessment | Aligned or Not Aligned? | Reasoning |
| Ms. Wen asks her students to read two different passages about the same topic and tell her which author’s style they like better and why. | Not aligned | Students’ preferences about author’s style does not show Ms. Wen if they understand what connector words are or how to use them to combine sentences. |
| Ms. Wen asks students to list connector words that can be combined to connect sentences | Aligned | Students will need to know these words to connect the sentences. Listing connector words will tell Ms. Wen whether her students have the necessary tools to combine sentences. |
