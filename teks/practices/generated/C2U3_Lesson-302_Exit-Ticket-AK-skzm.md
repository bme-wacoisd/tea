Answer the following questions about building trust and rapport with students and how rapport influences student learning.


How does building rapport with students have a positive impact on student learning?
Building rapport with students can have a positive impact on student learning because students feel safe in a trusted environment and are more likely to take academic risks and ask questions, which can ultimately improve their understanding of the class content.



What is authentic listening? How can it help build trust and rapport with students?

Authentic listening is a strategy to build rapport with students that includes the teacher giving full attention to conversations, demonstrating empathy, and suspending judgment so that the student can be fully heard. It can help build rapport with students by developing trust between students and the teacher, which is a result of having a positive, caring relationship. Authentic listening shows students that the teacher cares about what students have to say.
