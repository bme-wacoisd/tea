Which of the following is NOT an example of one of the guidelines for multiple means of action and expression?
Students have flexibility with time, pacing, and range of motor action required to interact with instruction materials.
Students to communicate using multiple forms of media.
The teacher supports students in developing reasonable plans for reaching their goals
Students share their learning through writing a summary
Why is this a nonexample of providing multiple means of action and expression?
All students share their learning in the same way – through a summary, which doesn’t provide alternatives for students who cannot or do not prefer responding in writing.
