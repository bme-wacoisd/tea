## Slide 4

### Vocabulary

- Concept Map: A visual organization and representation of knowledge. Concept maps show how topics and ideas relate to one another.
- Anticipation Guide: A set of statements about a topic that students react to (can be true/false statements, agree/disagree statements, etc.)
- Self Assessment: Assessments that students complete about their individual knowledge of a topic. Self-assessments are not graded but are used as a way for students to individually reflect on what they know about a given concept.
