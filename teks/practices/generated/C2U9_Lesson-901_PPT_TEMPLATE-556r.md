## Slide 4

### 4 Characteristics of Effective Feedback

- Specific and clear
- Focused on the task not the student
- Provides strengths and areas for improvement
- Timely and actionable

## Slide 5

### Vocabulary


| Informal Feedback | Feedback provided in the moment, not planned.  This feedback is tailored to a situation as it arises. |
| --- | --- |
| Formal Feedback | Feedback that is planned and systematically scheduled into the learning process.  It is provided consistently for each student. |
| Verbal Feedback | Immediate feedback provided to a student verbally. |
| Written Feedback | Formal feedback that is provided to as student in written form after a task has been completed. |
| Formative Feedback | Feedback that is provided during instruction and intended to move instruction or learning forward. |
| Summative Feedback | Feedback provided after task completion that is intended to provide information about where a student has met/not met success criteria for a task. |

- 5

## Slide 6

### Informal Feedback

- Delivered in the moment, tailored to the situation, not provided equally to all students
- During instruction/learning
- Verbally provided by the teacher
- Move learning/instruction forward, formative
- 6

---

## Slide 7

### Formal Feedback

- Planned and systematically scheduled into the learning process.  It is provided consistently for all students.
- Given after instruction or learning.
- Written or provided verbally by the teacher
- Purpose is to measure learning against standards, summative in nature.
- 7

---

## Slide 8

### Written Feedback

- Feedback that is provided to a student in written form after a task has been completed.
- Given after instruction or learning.
- Written, provided by the teacher
- Purpose is to provide a record of what a student is doing well, allows students to return to feedback to make revisions.
- 8

---

## Slide 9

### Verbal Feedback

- Immediate feedback provided during instruction.
- During instruction or sometimes after instruction.
- Verbal, provided by the teacher
- Allows for quick and instant correction; allows for a dialogue or clarification for student
- 9
