Practice: Collecting Quantitative and Qualitative Data about Students


| Type of Data | Definition | Questions Answered | Examples |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Quantitative Data | Data that can be counted and expressed in numbers | How many? How often? | Test scores and grades Number of times student has switched schools |
| Qualitative Data | Data that are expressed in words | Why? How? | Name of a student’s favorite book Student’s preferred learning activities |


| Because they each tell us something different. With both types of data, we’re more likely to see the whole picture about a student. |
