- Why is effective classroom management so important to student learning?

- Effective classroom management provides clarity and reasoning to students about how to participate in the classroom. It creates a safe environment because students understand what is expected of them. It reduces distractions and increases the amount of time for teaching and learning. It helps develop positive and trusting relationships between teachers and students and among students.





- What is the impact of ineffective classroom management on student outcomes?


- Lower grades, decreased likelihood of graduating high school, lower attendance, more likely to be suspended, more likely to be held back a grade, less economic gains, increased likelihood of juvenile and adult crime.
