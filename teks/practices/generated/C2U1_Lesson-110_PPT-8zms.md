## Slide 5

### Effective Delivery of Roll-Out Speeches

- Teachers show they are excited about the routine that students are learning.
- Teachers are clear in their communication with students.
- Teachers are concise. They say what they need to say, but they don’t confuse students with a lot of extra words.
