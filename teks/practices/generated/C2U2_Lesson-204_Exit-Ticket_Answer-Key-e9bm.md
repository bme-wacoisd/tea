With his lower pitch and slower pace, Mr. Baldwin is able to clearly articulate syllables and provide for distinction between his words.  At the same time, his back is straight, and his chin is up. While engaging students with a read-aloud and ensuing classroom discussion, Mr. Baldwin varies his volume, cadence, and pitch.  He exhibits a relaxed posture, maintains inconsistent eye contact, and makes sweeping hand gestures to hold his students’ attention.  Casual
