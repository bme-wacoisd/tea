## Slide 4

### Norms


| Norms for Teacher | Norms for role-playing students |
| --- | --- |
| Take teaching seriously and stay “in character.” Talk to your students in a grade appropriate way. Take time to prepare. Forgive yourself if at first it seems hard. | Take being a student seriously and stay “in character.” Actively respond to the teacher’s prompts and questions. Don’t act out or intentionally misbehave. Remember you are a third grader – the content may seem easy to you. Extend kindness to your teacher if at first they have trouble taking on the teaching role. |
