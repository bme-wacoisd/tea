## Slide 4

### Vocabulary

- 4

| Term | Definition |
| --- | --- |
| Research-Based Instructional Strategies | A set of practices supported by research that should be present in classrooms, regardless of instructional materials |
| Foundational Skills | A set of skills a student must master to become a skilled reader |
| Science of Reading | Settled science about how people learn to read |
| Structured Literacy | Evidence-based practices for teaching foundational literacy skills |
| Simple View of Reading | A formula demonstrating that to be a skilled reader, a learner must master two basic concepts: word recognition (decoding) and language compression (listening comprehension) |
| Word Recognition (Decoding) | Ability to apply sound-symbol relationships to read words |
| Language Comprehension | Ability to understand spoken words |

## Slide 5

### Research-Based Instructional Strategies

- 5

| RBIS are… | RBIS are not… |
| --- | --- |
| A set of research-based practices that highlights misconceptions common in the field | Topics that are commonly agreed upon (e.g., materials should be aligned to the standards) |
| Topics that require conceptual or philosophical shifts in approach to instruction | Topics not related to curriculum and instructional materials (e.g., classroom management best practices) |
| A set of practices that are supported by research and should be present in classrooms, regardless of instructional materials |  |
| A set of practices that relate directly to the design of instructional materials AND/OR the approach required to implement them well |  |

## Slide 6

### Content-Specific Practices for Reading Language Arts

- 6
- Foundational Skills: Explicit, systematic practice with literacy foundational skills
- Text Complexity: Regular practice with grade-level, complex text and its academic language
- Knowledge Coherence: Building knowledge and vocabulary through text in all content areas
- Text-Based Responses: Reading, writing, and speaking grounded in evidence from text, both literary and informational
