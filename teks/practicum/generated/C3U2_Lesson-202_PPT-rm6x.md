## Slide 6

### Professionalism Scenario #2

- Mr. Jarrell is a middle school math teacher. He just gave his students an assignment, but they seem to really be struggling. He doesn’t seem to understand because he just went over the assignment. Mr. Jarrell gives a signal for the students to direct their attention back to the board. He asks students to share what part of the assignment they are struggling with the most and models a few problems on the board before having students continue with the assignment.
- Professional
