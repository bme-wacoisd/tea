My Group’s Lever: Response will vary.
What does this lever mean?
(For strategic staffing) It means schools retain effective teachers by recruiting and selecting strong candidates and supporting them when they start teaching.
(For positive school culture) It means there is a clear school vision, mission, and goals; high expectations for student behavior; support for students; and engagement with families and the community.
(For high-quality instructional materials and assessments) It means students learn with resources aligned to the TEKS and take assessments that align with the TEKS.
(For effective instruction) It means teachers receive professional development as a regular part of their jobs and get the time to analyze data and tailor instruction to meet student needs.
What does a district do to support effective schools? List two action items.
(For strong school leadership and planning) Responses may vary but may include supporting the school leader and giving principals time to work on instruction in their schools.
(For strategic staffing) Responses may vary but may include giving schools control over the teachers they hire and encouraging effective teachers to work in low-performing schools.
(For positive school culture) Responses may vary but may include providing students with access to support services and providing tools for schools to engage families.
(For high-quality instructional materials and assessments) Responses may vary but may include providing schools with access to interim assessment data and high-quality instructional materials.
(For effective instruction) Responses may vary but may include providing schools with access to various data points on students and providing campus leaders with the training they need to support effective instruction.
What does a school do to support effective schools? List two action items.
(For strong school leadership and planning) Responses may vary but may include having strong improvement plans in place and having clear roles for the leaders in a school.
(For strategic staffing) Responses may vary but may include recruiting highly qualified educators and making sure they are supported.
(For positive school culture) Responses may vary but may include implementing surveys on culture and having a strong mission and vision.
(For high-quality instructional materials and assessments) Responses may vary but may include using high-quality daily lessons and materials that meet the needs of students with disabilities.
(For effective instruction) Responses may vary but may include using observations and feedback to help teachers improve and implementing effective classroom routines.
