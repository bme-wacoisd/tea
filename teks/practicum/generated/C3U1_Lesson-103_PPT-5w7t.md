## Slide 4

### Local Role in Education

- Operate Schools
- Implement and enforce state and federal laws
- Employ administrators, teachers, and support staff
- Provide funding for schools
- Determine how students learn state academic standards
- 4

---

## Slide 5

### Local School Board Responsibilities

- Set goals and priorities for the district and measure progress
- Adopt policies and review them regularly to determine when updates are needed
- Hire and evaluate the superintendent
- Set the district’s budget and the local tax rate
- Engage with the community
