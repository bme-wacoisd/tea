## Slide 4

### Vocabulary


| Term | Definition |
| --- | --- |
| Deliberate Practice | Practice that performs the learning goal (authentic), is spaced over time, is low-stakes, and is paired with feedback for improvement |
| Interactive Learning | Learning that requires active student participation |
| Modalities | Different forms of sensory perception |
| Outside of Text | Analyze the letter-sound relationships and parts of a single word in isolation |
| In Text | Students take the sounds they are practicing in isolation and read them within a text. |
| Decodable Text | A type of text that contains only the sound-letter relationships that have previously been taught. |
