# Do-It-Again


## Field-Based Experience: Practicum Teacher

### Tool Name: Do It Again

Name:
Date:
Objectives:
- Identify and describe strategies to establish a culture of excellence.
- Identify and describe the essential components of Do It Again
- Implement and reflect on strategies that build a culture of excellence.
- Discuss the relationship between high expectations, effective learning environments, classroom culture, and student learning.
Purpose:  The purpose of this tool is for students to observe how the Do It Again technique can be used to set the standard for quality in the class, while reinforcing classroom systems, routines, and culture.
Summary:  The tool includes a section for students to record their observations of the Do It Again technique. It also provides conversations starters for students to use with their field site campus teachers to learn more about how the reinforcement and maintenance of classroom systems, routines, and culture. Lastly, students are asked to practice planning and/or implementing the Do It Again technique, discuss feedback from the field site campus teacher, and reflect upon the experience and impact on teaching and learning.
