# Classroom-Rules


## Field-Based Experience: Practicum Teacher

### Tool Name: Classroom Rules

Name:
Date:
Objectives:
- Describe the characteristics of effective classroom rules.
- Analyze examples of classroom rules
- Develop a set of classroom rules that contribute to a safe and effective classroom environment.
Purpose:  The purpose of this tool is for students to observe the characteristics of effective classroom rules and the impact on instruction.
Summary:  The tool provides a section for students to record their observations about classroom rules, sectioned by columns for characteristics of effectiveness:  simplicity, clarity, and student ownership. It also provides conversations starters for students to use with their field site campus teachers to learn more about how classroom rules impact the field site campus teacher’s ability to implement instruction, support consistency, and promote high expectations for all students. Lastly, the tool prompts students to develop a script to introduce classroom rules, receive feedback from the field site campus teacher, and reflect upon the experience of introducing or reinforcing classroom rules.
