## Slide 4

### Vocabulary


| Stress | How our body and mind respond to challenging situations |
| --- | --- |
| Distress | Stressful situations with negative effects on a person’s mental, emotional, or physical health |
| Eustress | Stressful situations with positive effects on a person’s mental, emotional, or physical health |
| Work-Life Balance | The ratio of time a person spends on job-related tasks (at work or at home) versus the amount of time a person spends on leisure activities and spending quality time with friends, family, and/or hobbies |
| Boundaries | Clearly communicated expectations for how a person interacts with others |

## Slide 5

### Dealing with Stress: Healthy & Unhealthy Strategies

- Healthy Stress Management
- Exercise
- Getting enough sleep
- Talking to a friend
- Craft/hobbies
- Spending time with pets/animals
- Outdoor activities
- Volunteering
- Unhealthy Stress Management
- Not eating/overeating
- Not getting enough sleep
- Substance use
- Not asking for help
- Fighting/arguing with others
- 5

---

## Slide 6

### Examples of Boundaries for Teachers

- Establishing due dates and enforcing them
- Limiting communication with parents, students or colleagues after school hours or on weekends
- Not bringing papers home to grade/limiting work doen from home
- Only volunteering for extra duties when they have time/not overbooking their schedule
