## Slide 4

### Vocabulary

- Long-term Professional Growth: The process by which educators gain new skills, seek opportunities and career experiences to continuously improve their practice and knowledge to benefit both their short and long-term goals.
- 4

---

## Slide 5

### What are Professional Learning Organizations?

- Organizations that provide courses or training to help teachers develop the necessary knowledge and skills to be successful practitioners. They promote long-term professional growth by providing growth opportunities.
- 5

---

## Slide 6

### What are Professional Growth Opportunities?

- The various modalities teachers can receive long-term professional growth including coursework, advanced degrees, certifications, workshops, career exchanges, scholarships, research
- 6

---

## Slide 7

### Where are Professional Learning Organizations?

- Local, State, and National
- 7

## Slide 8

### Why Do Teachers Use/Seek Them?

- Continue developing skills and knowledge
- Maintain required hours necessary for licensing
- Increase earning potential
- Long-term professional growth
- Acknowledgement as a teacher
- 8

---

## Slide 9

### What are Some Examples of Organizations & Opportunities?

- Local: Higher education institutions (universities, colleges, community colleges), museums, centers of education, art institutes.
- State: Teacher associations, TEA, Education Nonprofits.
- National: Fulbright, National Board Certification, Teacher Plus Scholarship, TNTP
