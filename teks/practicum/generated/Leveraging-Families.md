# Leveraging-Families


## Field-Based Experience: Practicum Teacher

### Tool Name: Engaging Families

Name:
Date:
Objectives:
- Explain different methods of communication with students’ families.
- Identify barriers to effectively communicating with students’ families.
- Use a family communication tracking log to document family contact.
Purpose:  The purpose of this tool is for students to observe the importance of frequent and consistent communication with students’ families.
Summary:  The tool provides a section for students to record their observations of the field site campus teacher’s practices for communicating with students’ families. It also provides conversation starters for students to use with their field site campus. Lastly, students assist in soliciting preferred methods of communication with students’ families, creating a Family Communication Tracking log, recording, and cataloguing communication with students’ families, and/or drafting a message to parents to celebrate a recent classroom success.
Connections to Instructional Practices:
Lesson 3.09:  Engaging Families to Improve Student Outcomes
