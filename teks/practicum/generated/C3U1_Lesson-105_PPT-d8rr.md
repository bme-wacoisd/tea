## Slide 4

### Teacher Roles

- Academic Leader: Set high expectations, demonstrate curiosity and enthusiasm for learning, have a deep content knowledge, prioritize student learning
- Behavioral Leader: Demonstrate ethical and professional behavior, model kindness and fairness toward all students, strive to develop positive relationships with all students
- Reflective Leader: Continually engage in improving teaching practices, share knowledge and collaborate with colleagues, ask for feedback on teaching practices
