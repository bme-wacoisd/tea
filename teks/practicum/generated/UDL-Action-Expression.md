# UDL-Action-Expression


## Field-Based Experience: Practicum Teacher

### Tool Name: Universal Design for Learning | Action & Expression

Name:
Date:
Objectives:
Identify and analyze the use of the Universal Design for Learning guidelines in a lesson.
Evaluate the impact of using the Universal Design for Learning guidelines on learning.
Purpose:  The purpose of this tool is for students to observe the Universal Design for Learning Guideline of providing multiple means of action and expression to support learning for all students.
Summary:  The tool provides a section for students to record their observations of how the field site campus teacher uses the Universal Design for Learning Guidelines to provide multiple means of action and expression during instruction. It also provides conversation starters for students to use with their field site campus teacher. Lastly, students practice planning and/or implementing an instructional activity that provides multiple means of action and expression.
