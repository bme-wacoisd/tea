| Vocabulary | Goal-Setting and Professional Development (GSPD) Process | Goal-Setting and Professional Development (GSPD) Process | An on-going, recursive process where teachers reflect on current professional practices, identify professional growth goals, establish a professional development plan to attain those goals, and reflect on goal attainment. |
| Vocabulary | Professional Development Plan | Professional Development Plan | A detailed plan for how a teacher will attain their professional growth goals and track their progress toward the goals over the course of the year. |
| Vocabulary | SMART Goal | SMART Goal | An acronym for goal setting that stands for Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, and Timely |

| 30 mins | Developing a Professional Development Plan  T: In our previous lesson, you had the opportunity to interview an educator to learn more about strategies and resources to help you achieve your professional goals. Now, you are going to use the information you gained from the interview to help you develop a professional development plan for how you will attain your professional growth goals.  T: It is also important for a teacher to think about how they will know if the goal has been met. A teacher must consider how they will track their progress toward their goals, the evidence they will use to determine if they have met the goal, and how they will know whether their performance has impacted instruction and student achievement.   T: To develop your plan, you will need to think about the following questions:  SMART Goal â€“ What are your goals? What do you want to achieve? Actions - How will you accomplish the goal? How will you know whether it has impacted instruction and student achievement?  T: Your professional development plan should include the following success criteria:  At least 2 professional growth goals A cohesive plan to attain each goal A plan for how you will track progress toward each goal throughout the year. S:  The process allows the educator to work on improving in areas of their interest or need.
