Notes: Importance of First Impressions
1. What does it mean: “You don’t get a second chance at first impressions”?
Introductions and first impressions are important. They set a tone and a baseline for your relationship or partnership
2. Why is a teacher introducing themselves to their students important?
A teacher introducing themselves to their students is important because it helps set the tone for the class and it helps the students feel welcome and warm in the class, sets the expectations for the year, and gives students a chance to get to know you, feel comfortable, and familiar with you.
3. What are the three components of a teacher’s introduction?
Creating a warm and welcoming environment
Classroom Expectations and Rules, Grading and Class Goals
Personal Introduction/Background

4. What makes an effective introductory speech?
An effective introductory speech is clearly and thoroughly communicated, and it leaves a positive and lasting impression on the students.
