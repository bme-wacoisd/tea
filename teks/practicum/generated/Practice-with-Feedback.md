# Practice-with-Feedback


## Field-Based Experience: Practicum Teacher

### Tool Name: Meaningful Questioning

Name:
Date:
Objectives:
Understand why practice is vital to learning.
Evaluate the qualities of deliberate practice.
Identify characteristics of quality feedback
Purpose:  The purpose of this tool is to for students to observe how practice with feedback can engage students’ working memory and move ideas and content to long-term memory.
Summary:  The tool provides a section for students to record their observations of how the teacher uses practice with feedback. It also provides conversation starters for students to use with their field site campus teacher. Lastly, students practice planning and/or implementing an instructional activity that intentionally incorporates practice with feedback to engage students’ working memory and move ideas and content to long-term memory.
