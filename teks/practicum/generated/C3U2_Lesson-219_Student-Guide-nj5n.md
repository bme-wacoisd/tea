Model: Researching Professional Learning Organizations and Long-Term Professional Growth Opportunities
Long-term professional opportunities can look very different for different organizations. There are many benefits to participating in long-term professional growth opportunities – but the most important is to find professional growth opportunities that fit the interests and needs of the individual teacher.
Where and How to Search
Texas Education Agency Approved CPE Providers
Examples to research: Fulbright (choose a different program), National Boards, Teacher Plus Scholarship, TNTP
Google Search > “Professional Learning Organizations in Texas”
Google Search > “Higher Education” + “Professional Learning Organizations” + “Texas”
Google Search > “National” + “Professional Learning Organizations”
Research Table Instructions
1. Identify an organization > Choose one from the list. Is it local (for the city or region), is it statewide, or is it national?
2. Click on the organization > Go to the website and navigate to the “About” or “Mission” page
3. Find the “Mission Statement” > Insert the mission or information about the institution in the table
4. Find Programming > Find various programs and choose one or two programs, read about them, and summarize them in the table.
5. Find Requirements > Look for eligibility or requirements to participate in programming and summarize them in the table.
