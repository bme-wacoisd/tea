Austin State University Charter school and found their teacher requirements on their website, shown below:
Teachers must have, at minimum, a bachelor's degree with appropriate and valid Texas certification in the area required. A degree emphasis in early childhood education is preferred. Additionally, two years of effective classroom teaching experience is preferred.

List the steps Jia must follow to be considered qualified to work at Stephen F. Austin State University Charter School.
Jia must:
Earn her degree in early childhood education from her teacher preparation program.
Pass teacher certification exam in content area.
Apply for certification with the state.
Pass a background check.
