### Tool Name: Information About Students

Date:
Objectives:
- Explain different methods of discerning students’ funds of knowledge.
- Plan a family interview to discern a student’s funds of knowledge and consider implications for teaching and learning.
Purpose:  The purpose of this tool is for students to observe how students’ funds of knowledge are used in making instructional decisions.
Summary:  The tool provides a section for students to record their observations of how the field site campus teacher uses their knowledge of students to design instruction. It also provides conversation starters for students to use their field site campus teacher. Lastly, students design a question set for a family interview.
Connections to Instructional Practices:
Lesson 3.10:  Using Information About Students from Families to Make Instructional Decisions
