## Slide 4

### Vocabulary


| Term | Definition |
| --- | --- |
| Text complexity | The level of difficulty in reading and understanding a text. The level of difficulty is based on the readability of the text, the levels of meaning or purpose in the text, the clarity and conventions of the language, the knowledge demands, and the structure of the text. |
| Academic Language | The specialized language, written and spoken, in classroom or other academic locations that helps students communicate and think about what they are learning. |
| Implicit meaning | Something in a text is not stated outright or directly but is inferred. |

## Slide 10

### Structure of the Text

- Text structure refers to how the text is organized in regard to elements such as viewpoint, multiple characters, storylines, and complex details.
- Text that are organized chronologically tend to be easier to understand than those that use flashbacks or varied time and sequence.
