## Slide 3

### Bellringer

- A teacher is concerned about several of her students who are learning English as a second language. They are falling behind in class and becoming frustrated. She speaks with a representative from a local tutoring program. The program is free and offers quality after-school tutoring for students struggling in reading. The representative asks for more information about the students, including their recent state test scores. The teacher has reservations about sharing this information but decides it's in the student's best interest.
- Is this teacher acting ethically?
