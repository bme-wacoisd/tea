## Slide 4

### Vocabulary


| Term | Definition |
| --- | --- |
| Cold Assessments | Assessments that include texts and topics the students have not read or are unfamiliar with. These types of assessments are usually multiple-choice. |
| Warm Assessments | Assessments that include texts that students have not read, but on related topics or related texts. These types of assessments can be multiple choice or open-ended and are usually embedded in the curriculum. |
| Hot Assessments | Assessments that involve familiar texts that students have read and studied and are usually open-ended questions. These assessments are also embedded in the curriculum. |
| Curriculum Embedded Tasks | Tasks or assessments students are completing as part of the curriculum. |
