| Scenario | Approach |

| The teacher teaches a mini lesson on captions and how they help students understand the pictures they accompany, including a think aloud of the connections she makes in her mind when she encounters a caption. Next, students read non-fiction books about other types of whales, completing a graphic organizer to capture key facts about the animals as they read. Students then include these facts in a writing assignment later in the lesson. | Knowledge-based |
