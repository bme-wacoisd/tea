## Slide 4

### Encouraging Productive Struggle through the Use of High-Quality Instructional Materials


| 1 | High-quality instructional materials ask students to engage with grade-level content, which ensures that there are high expectations for students to meet. |
| --- | --- |
| 2 | High-quality instructional materials also give students opportunities to engage with the content in different ways. As we discussed in the previous lessons, one of the ways that students can struggle productively is by trying to solve problems in different ways. |
| 3 | When a teacher plans to use high-quality instructional materials, part of the planning process can focus on identifying the misconceptions that students might have and how they can support them with struggling productively through those misconceptions. |
