How do adults learn new information and skills once they finish school?
They read books, they take a class, they learn new things on-the-job at work.

What skills that K-12 teachers hold would be beneficial for someone hoping to teach adults? What additional skills might they need?
Similar skills: Understanding teaching strategies, knowing how to adapt learning to meet the learner’s needs
Different skills: Need to know how to work with adults instead of children. They might also need to know more challenging or very specific content.
