# Examples-Nonexamples


## Field-Based Experience: Practicum Teacher

### Tool Name: Examples & Nonexamples

Name:
Date:
Objectives:
Identify and analyze the use of examples and nonexamples in a lesson.
- Evaluate the impact of using examples and nonexamples on learning.
Purpose:  The purpose of this tool is for students to observe how examples and nonexamples in a lesson help students recognize a concept or idea in a variety of contexts and develop and accurate mental picture of what the concept is and is not.
Summary:  The tool provides a section for students to record their observations of how the field site campus teacher uses examples and nonexamples in a lesson. It also provides conversation starters for students to use with their field site campus teacher. Lastly, students practice planning and/or implementing an instructional activity that uses examples and nonexamples.
