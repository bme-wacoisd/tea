## Slide 4

### Vocabulary

- Federalism: A form of government in which power is divided between state and national levels.
- Federal: (when used to describe a country’s type of government) describes a nation in which the power is divided between state and national levels.
- Federal: (when used in the United States to describe a law, organization, or level of government) describes something associated with the national level of government.

## Slide 5

### Federal Governance of Education in the US

- 10th Amendment: The federal government only has control in areas specifically mentioned in the Constitution.
- 14th Amendment: No state can make a law that denies anyone of “life, liberty, and property.”
- The federal government has less control than states and districts over what happens in schools.
