# Positive-Framing


## Field-Based Experience: Practicum Teacher

### Tool Name: Positive Framing

Name:
Date:
Objectives:
- Analyze and apply the strategy of Positive Framing for building positive relationships with students.
- Identify and describe the essential components of Positive Framing
- Practice drafting a redirection statement using Positive Framing

Purpose:  The purpose of this tool is for students to identify the key components of Positive Framing as a way to frame interactions, particularly corrections, in a way that reinforces trust, while still guiding students toward a better course of action.
Summary:  The tool provides a section for students to record their observations about effective behavior management techniques.  It also provides conversations starters for students to use with their field site campus teachers to learn more about the essential components of Positive Framing as a way to frame interactions, particularly corrections, in a manner that reinforces trust, while still guiding students toward a better course of action.  Lastly, students are asked to practice Positive Framing.
