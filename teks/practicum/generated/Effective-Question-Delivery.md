# Effective-Question-Delivery


## Field-Based Experience: Practicum Teacher

### Tool Name: Effective Question Delivery

Name:
Date:
Objectives:
- Observe and reflect on the characteristics of effective question delivery and response.
- Practice and evaluate question delivery and response.
Purpose: The purpose of this tool is for students to observe the effective delivery of questions and response to student answers.
Summary: The tool provides a section for students to record their observations of the delivery of questions and the response to student answers. It also provides students the opportunity to apply their knowledge by effectively delivering questions and responding to student answers during a lesson within the field site teacher’s unit of study and receiving feedback. Lastly, the tool prompts students to reflect upon their learning experience about the importance of each characteristic of effective question delivery.
