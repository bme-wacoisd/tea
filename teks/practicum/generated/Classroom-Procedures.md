# Classroom-Procedures


## Field-Based Experience: Practicum Teacher

### Tool Name: Classroom Procedures

Name:
Date:
Objectives:
- Develop a classroom management plan that addresses procedures and routines for the beginning and end of class as well as managing work during class.

Purpose:  The purpose of this tool is for students to identify the key components of effective classroom management.
Summary:  The tool provides a section for students to record their observations about classroom management, routines, and procedures. It also provides students the opportunity to apply their knowledge about effective assessments by reviewing or creating a classroom management plan. Lastly, the tool prompts students to implement classroom procedures, receive feedback from the field site campus teacher, and reflect upon the experience of engaging with beginning of class routines, managing work routines, and end of class routines.
