- How do you determine text complexity?
- Text contains more implicit meaning and uses unconventional structures.
- Uses figurative language, ambiguity, archaic or unfamiliar language (academic or domain specific)
- Uses complex sentence structure and vocabulary.

- Why does text complexity matter?
- Complex texts provide students opportunities to work with new language, knowledge, and ways of thinking.
- Complex texts are more rigorous and help students to learn faster.
- Students must learn to read complex texts so they can get into college and be able to write more effectively.
- All of the above.

- What is the role of text complexity in effective literacy instruction?
- Students need regular practice with grade-level, complex text, and its academic language to grow in their reading abilities. They need frequent exposure to challenging vocabulary and knowledge.
