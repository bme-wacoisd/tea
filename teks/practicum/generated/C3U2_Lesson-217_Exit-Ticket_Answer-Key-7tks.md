### Lesson 2.17: Presenting a Stress Management Plan

- Why is planning for, recognizing, and managing job-related stress and important part of a successful career as an educator?
It is evident that teacher burnout is a common reason why people leave the profession. Seeing the signs early and addressing them with an effective plan could allow more teachers to remain in the profession and doing something that they love.

- What are some of the key stressors that you will need to plan for as a teacher?
- Having to complete extracurricular activities like coaching a sport or advising a club
- Large class sizes
- Lack of funding for materials to optimize the learning environment
- Paperwork – grading papers, completing progress reports, etc.
- Attending meetings – staff meeting, PLCs, committee meetings, IEP meetings
- Complicated lesson plan templates


- What were some of the strategies that you heard from your peers that you might incorporate into your plan to manage stress?
Setting boundaries
Taking time to rest
Using time management skills
Eating Healthy
Creating a work-life balance
Finding a supportive network
