# Authentic-Listening


## Field-Based Experience: Practicum Teacher

### Tool Name: Positive Framing

Name:
Date:
Objectives:
- Identify and describe how building trust and rapport with students can lead to a positive impact on student learning.
- Practice authentic listening as a strategy to build trust and rapport with students.

Purpose:  The purpose of this tool is to provide students with the opportunity to identify the key components of authentic listening to build trust and rapport with students in a way that can lead to a positive impact on student learning.
Summary:  The tool provides a section for students to record their observations about authentic listening, sectioned by success criteria rows. It also provides conversation starters for students to use with their field site campus teachers. Lastly, students practice the implementation of authentic learning success criteria during a check-in with a student and reflect upon the impact on trust, rapport, and student learning.
