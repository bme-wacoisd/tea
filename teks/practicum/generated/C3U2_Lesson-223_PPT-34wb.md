## Slide 4

### Vocabulary


| Goal-Setting and Professional Development (GSPD) Process | An on-going, recursive process where teachers reflect on current professional practices, identify professional growth goals, establish a professional development plan to attain those goals, and reflect on goal attainment. |
| --- | --- |
| Professional Development Plan | A detailed plan for how a teacher will attain their professional growth goals and track their progress toward the goals over the course of the year. |
| SMART Goal | An acronym for goal setting that stands for Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, and Timely |

- 4

## Slide 5

### Considerations When Developing Your Plan

- SMART Goal – What are your goals? What do you want to achieve?
- Actions - How will you accomplish the goal? What strategies and resources will you use to attain the goal?
- Evidence of Goal Attainment - How will you know your goal has been met? How will you know whether it has impacted instruction and student achievement?
- 5

---

## Slide 6

### Professional Development Plan Success Criteria

- At least 2 professional growth goals
- A cohesive plan to attain each goal
- A plan for how you will track progress toward each goal throughout the year.
