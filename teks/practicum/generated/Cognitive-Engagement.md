## Field-Based Experience: Practicum Teacher

### Tool Name: Cognitive Engagement

Name:
Date:
Objectives:
- Evaluate the impact of cognitive engagement on learning.
Purpose:  The purpose of this tool is for students to observe the impact of cognitive engagement on learning.
Summary:  The tool provides a section for students to record their observations of how the field site campus cognitively engages students. It also provides conversation starters for students to use with their field site campus teacher. Lastly, students  practice planning and/or implementing an instructional activity.
