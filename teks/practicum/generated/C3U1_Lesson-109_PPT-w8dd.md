## Slide 4

### Vocabulary

- Hard to Staff: Teaching category (such as special education, bilingual education, mathematics, or science) in which there is an indication of multi-year data to indicate a teacher shortage in the area
- Teacher Shortage: Occurs when there are not enough teachers to staff open positions
- Teacher Attrition: The rate at which teachers are exiting the field of education each year
- Teacher Recruitment: Efforts by schools and districts to attract qualified individuals to the field of education.
