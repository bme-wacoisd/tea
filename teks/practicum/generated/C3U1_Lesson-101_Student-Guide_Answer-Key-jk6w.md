Practicum in Education and Training

Practicum: a learning experience that provides students with the opportunity to apply what they have learned in a real-life situation.

Course Units
Unit 1: Career Exploration and Awareness
Explore your own approach to teaching, but also learn more about the profession in general, including trends in the teacher workforce and how education in the United States is governed.
Unit 2: Professionalism and Ethics
Explore the Texas Code of Ethics for Educators and consider how educators need to act professionally with their students and colleagues and as role models within their communities.
Unit 3: Research-Based Instructional Strategies in English Language Arts
How teachers of all content areas can support students with building literacy skills.

Unit 4: Research-Based Instructional Strategies in Mathematics
Instruction in mathematics and how teachers can build students’ numeracy skills.
