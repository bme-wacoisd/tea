# Challenge-Talk-Aspirations


## Field-Based Experience: Practicum Teacher

### Tool Name: Challenge & Talk Aspirations

Name:
Date:
Objectives:
- Identify and apply effective behavior management techniques.
- Practice motivating and inspiring students through Challenges and Talk Aspirations

Purpose:  The purpose of this tool is for students to learn how to Challenge students and use Talk Aspirations to motivate and inspire them to sustain or improve their performance.
The purpose of this tool is for students to identify the key components of Positive Framing to frame interactions, particularly corrections, in a way that reinforces trust, while still guiding students toward a better course of action.
Summary:  The tool provides a section for students to record their observations about effective behavior management techniques. It also provides conversations starters for students to use with their field site campus teachers to learn more about how the observed conflict resolution strategies such as Positive Framing, de-escalation, and emotional neutrality contribute to effective behavior management. Lastly, students are asked to practice Positive Framing.
