### Lesson 2.14: What is Teacher Burnout?

Name:
Date:
True or False:
- Burnout is a state of chronic stress that leads to physical and emotional exhaustion.
- Burnout is unique to teachers and only happens because of the unique stressors teachers face.
- Burnout can cause teachers to feel detached, ineffective, cynical, and unaccomplished.
- Stressors that can cause burnout include school organization, job demands, work resources, and teacher social and emotional competences.
- Warning signs of burnout are feeling tired and unhappy for a short period of time.


- Which of the following is not a warning sign of burning out?
- Exhaustion, fatigue, and difficulty sleeping.
- Feeling depressed and withdrawn from others.
- Frustrated after a class behaves poorly.
- Physical symptoms like headaches, stomachaches, racing heart.
