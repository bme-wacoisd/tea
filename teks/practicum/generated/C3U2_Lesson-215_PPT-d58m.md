## Slide 4

### Vocabulary

- Burnout: A state of chronic stress that leads to physical and emotional exhaustion, cynicism, detachment, and feelings of infectiveness and lack of accomplishment.
- 4

---

## Slide 5

### Systemic Supports to Prevent Burnout

- Personal or physical strategies to prepare a teacher for challenges, as well as tools, resources, and structures in place in a school or district to help teachers and make them feel a culture of trust and support in their professional setting.
- 5

---

## Slide 6

### Why do school systems have systemic supports?

- Professional environments and organizational systems can sometimes cause teacher burnout. Schools create systems of support to prevent burnout, help teachers feel respected and trusted, and to help teachers find ways to solve problems early-on.
- 6

---

## Slide 7

### Examples of Systemic Supports

- Teacher preparation (training and material resources), mentoring programs, and teacher collaboration.
- 7

---

## Slide 8

### Burnout Prevention


| Prevention Type | Description | How it prevents burnout |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Preparation (Training and Material Resources) | Training and materials to support student needs or for topics that impact students like mental health, mindfulness, trauma, response to intervention, behavior management systems, successful teaching strategies, organizational strategies, etc. | Teachers feel prepared and capable of dealing with the challenges presented by students and in the classroom. |
| Mentoring Programs | Teachers have mentor teachers or a mentorship program that provides support in a comprehensive and long-term way (instructional support, classroom management support, emotional support, etc.) | It provides a sense of connection, interaction, and communication that helps teachers not feel alone in their struggles or challenges. |
| Teacher Collaboration | Teachers working together to plan lessons or units of instruction, create classroom management tools, discuss instructional methods, discuss classroom challenges or successes, etc. | Supports effective instruction, reduction of workload, and sense of connection and interaction. |
