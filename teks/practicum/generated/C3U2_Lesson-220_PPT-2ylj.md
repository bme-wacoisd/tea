## Slide 4

### Vocabulary


| Professional Learning Communities | A group of educators that meet regularly to share expertise and works collaboratively to improve teaching skills and the academic performance of students. |
| --- | --- |
| Peer Observation | Observing the practice of another teacher to learn from one another not to formally evaluate the teacher. |
| Action Research | Form of investigation used by teachers to attempt to solve problems and improve professional practices in their classroom. |
| Lesson Study | Collaborative model where teachers plan a lesson together, teach and observe the lesson, and reflect on the success of the lesson. |

## Slide 6

### Why do school and district-based systems for teacher growth exist?

- They help support student success and growth to be ready for post-secondary life. Systems of teacher growth are meant to align to student achievement and benefit the performance of students. They are supportive, job-embedded, focused on instruction, continuous, and collaborative
