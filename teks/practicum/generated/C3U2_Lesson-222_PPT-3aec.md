## Slide 6

### Interview Preparation

- Share your goals.
- Share how you plan to achieve these goals.
- What strategies could I use to achieve my goals?
- What organizations, conferences, and/or trainings could I join to help me achieve my goals?
- What resources or additional information might I need?
- Write additional questions you might want to ask an educator that will help you develop a professional development plan.
