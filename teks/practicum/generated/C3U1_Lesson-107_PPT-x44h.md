## Slide 4

### Vocabulary

- Traditional Schools: A school operated and managed by a local district that is publicly funded, is open to all students, and is held to state standards.
- Charter Schools: A publicly funded independent school established under the terms of a charter (or contract) with the State of Texas. These schools have increased flexibility but can lose their charter if they do not meet set accountability standards.
- Private Schools: Independent, schools operated by private groups or individuals that are funded without the assistance of local, state, or federal governments.
