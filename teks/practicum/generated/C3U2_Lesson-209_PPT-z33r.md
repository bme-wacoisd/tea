## Slide 6

### SBEC Disciplinary Measures

- Place restrictions on issuing, renewing, or holding of an educator’s certificate either indefinitely or for a set term
- Issue an inscribed or non-inscribed reprimand
- Suspend a certificate for a set term or issue a probated suspension for a set term
- Revoke or cancel a certificate without opportunity for reapplications for a set term or permanently
- Impose any additional conductions or restrictions deemed necessary by SBEC.
