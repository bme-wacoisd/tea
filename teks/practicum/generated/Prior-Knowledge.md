# Prior-Knowledge


## Field-Based Experience: Practicum Teacher

### Tool Name: Prior Knowledge

Name:
Date:
Objectives:
- Define and describe the role of prior knowledge in learning.
Purpose:  The purpose of this tool is to provide students with the opportunity to observe the importance of prior knowledge in new learning.
Summary:  The tool provides a section for students to record their observations of how the field site campus teacher analyzes students’ prior knowledge and misconceptions as an essential component of instructional decision-making. It also provides conversation starters for students to use with their field site campus teacher. Lastly, students practice and reflect upon the planning and/or implementation of a prior knowledge activity.
Connections to Instructional Practices:
Lesson 4.05:  Prior Knowledge | Practice
Lesson 4.06:  Avoiding Overload | Too Much Information Can Interfere with Learning
