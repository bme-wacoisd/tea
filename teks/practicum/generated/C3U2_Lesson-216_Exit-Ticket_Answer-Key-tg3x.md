### Lesson 2.16: Stress Management Planning

How can having a personal stress management plan support you in maintaining good mental health as a teacher?
A stress management plan helps you to think through those areas that could potentially be at risk for stress and how you would deal with them. Being able to recognize stressors in your life and having a plan to deal with them can make you happier, healthier, and more effective/productive as a teacher.
