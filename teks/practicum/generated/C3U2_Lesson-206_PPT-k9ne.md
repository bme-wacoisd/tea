## Slide 5

### Steps of a Productive Parent Teacher Conference

- Greet the parents and establish a common purpose for the conference.
- Invite the parents to share their thoughts on how the student is doing.
- Share positive feedback about the student.
- Share no more than 2 areas for improvement.
- Setting a goal for the student with the parents.
- Ask parents if they have any questions.
