### Lesson 3.06: Complex Text in Tier 1 Instruction

- What is tier one Instruction?
- Instruction using a research-based core program or curriculum focused on essential grade-level skills and concepts.

- Why is it important to provide students opportunities to access grade-level complex texts during tier one instruction?
- All students should have access to engage with complex texts because it helps the student grow their vocabulary, knowledge, and oral language skills. They can also develop the skills to access complex text independently.

- What supports and scaffolds can a teacher provide to support students in accessing grade-level complex texts?
To help students access grade-level complex texts, teachers can:
- Pre-teach unfamiliar vocabulary and concepts.
- Provide necessary background knowledge.
- Use Text Sets (multiple texts on the same topic)
- Use graphic organizers.
- Read complex texts aloud to students.
