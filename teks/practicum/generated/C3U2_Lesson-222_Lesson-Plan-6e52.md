| 5 mins | Professional Development Plan  T: In the previous lesson, we learned that teachers continue to learn and grow well after they are certified. There are many and varied opportunities to improve your knowledge and practice and having goals and a plan will help you continue to improve and grow throughout your career. Today, we want to work on creating a plan to help you achieve the goals you developed.  T: Once goals are established, a teacher will develop a professional development plan (PDP).  T: What is a professional development plan?  S: A detailed plan for how a teacher will attain their professional growth goals and track their progress toward the goals over the course of the year.  T: Let’s look back at Kia’s goal around classroom management.  Goal: I will learn new ways to effectively manage collaborative groups to increase student productivity and learning.  T: What are the specific actions needed for Kia’s goal to be accomplished?  S: She can talk to other teachers about how they structure their groups. She can visit other classrooms. She can attend professional development. She can research new strategies online.   T: Those are great strategies. Kia determined that she would complete the following actions to help her grow in this area:  Visit other classrooms during collaborative groups to see how other teachers structure their groups Attend a professional development on classroom management Collaborate with other teammates to learn new strategies and processes for effectively managing groups. | Student Guide |
| 5 mins | Interview Preparation T: Now, you are going to talk with an educator to determine specific actions and strategies for achieving the goals you created in our previous lesson.   T: Let’s look at some questions you might want to ask during the interview: Share your goals. Share how you plan to achieve these goals. What strategies could I use to achieve my goals? What organizations, conferences, and/or trainings could I join to help me achieve my goals? What resources or additional information might I need? T: What additional questions might you want to ask an educator that will help you develop a professional development plan? S: Student responses will vary. T: Take a few minutes to add any additional questions before connecting with the teacher you are going to interview. S: (Write additional questions in student guide). | Student Guide Recording Device (optional) |
