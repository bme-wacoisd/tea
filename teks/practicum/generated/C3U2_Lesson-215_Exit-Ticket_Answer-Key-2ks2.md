### Lesson 2.15: Preventing Teacher Burnout

Name:
Date:
What are three systemic supports for teachers that can help to prevent burnout?  What makes them effective in preventing burnout?

Focus on preparation through training and provide key physical resources:  Trainings on topics that impact students (mental health, mindfulness, trauma) can help teachers to feel more prepared and capable of dealing with the challenges.

Teacher Collaboration:  Supports effective instruction, reduction in workload and sense of connection and interaction.

Mentoring Programs: Support teachers in comprehensive, long-term programs provides a sense of connection, interaction and communication that helps teacher to not feel alone in their struggles.
