### Vocabulary


| Professionalism | The qualities, behaviors, and attitudes a person exhibits on the job. |
| --- | --- |
| Ethical behavior | Respecting and obeying the law, being honest and demonstrating good moral character and being fair to all. |
| Conduct | The way a person behaves. |

## Slide 5

### Areas of Teacher Professionalism

- Interactions with Students: How a teacher engages and communicates with students
- Workplace Professional Conduct: How a teacher behaves at work
- Appearance: How a teacher visually presents themselves
- Interactions with Colleagues and the Community: How a teacher engages and communicates with colleagues, parents, and other members of the community
