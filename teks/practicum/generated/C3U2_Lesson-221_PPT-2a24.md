## Slide 4

### Vocabulary


| Goal-Setting and Professional Development (GSPD) Process | An on-going, recursive process where teachers reflect on current professional practices, identify professional growth goals, establish a professional development plan to attain those goals, and reflect on goal attainment. |
| --- | --- |
| Professional Development Plan | A detailed plan for how a teacher will attain their professional growth goals and track their progress toward the goals over the course of the year. |
| SMART Goal | An acronym for goal setting that stands for Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, and Timely |

## Slide 5

### Goal Setting and Professional Development (GSPD) Process

- This process is designed to help teachers:
- reflect on their current professional practices
- identify their professional growth goals
- build their professional development plan to attain those goals
- track their progress toward their goals over the course of the year

## Slide 6

### SMART Goals

- Specific - What do you want to accomplish?
- Measurable - How will you know when your goal is met?
- Achievable- What steps can you take to reach your goal?
- Relevant - How will meeting this goal benefit you and your students?
- Timely – What is the end date for your goal?
