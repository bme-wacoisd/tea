- In your own words, describe the three steps to planning and implementing a just-in-time intervention.

- First, the teacher needs to identify what gaps exist for students in terms of knowledge needed to complete the grade-level work.
- Second, they need to figure out when they should give the intervention, based on their scope and sequence or unit plan.
- Then they need to create the materials for the intervention and provide it to students.
