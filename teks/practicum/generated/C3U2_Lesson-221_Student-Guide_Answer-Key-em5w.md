Introduction to the Goal-Setting and Professional Development (GSPD) Process

The GSPD process is designed for a teacher to:

reflect on their current professional practices
identify their professional growth goals
build their professional development plan to attain those goals
track their progress toward their goals over the course of the year

Goal Development

Questions to consider when developing SMART goals:

Specific - What do you want to accomplish?
Measurable - How will you know when you goal is met?
Achievable	- What steps can you take to reach your goal?
Relevant - How will meeting this goal benefit you and your students?
Timely – What is the end date for your goal?




Practice
